Embarking on the counselling journey can be significant, demonstrating your commitment to personal growth. Ensuring that you find the right therapist is important, so to help you take this step, I offer a free consultation, as I believe that trust and genuine connection are the cornerstones of a meaningful therapeutic relationship. This is an opportunity for you to express your counselling goals, ask any questions, and see if we would like to work together.

I offer flexible therapy options designed to fit into your schedule. Alongside face-to-face sessions, I provide online and telephone counselling, allowing you to access professional support from the comfort of your own space, at a time that works for you. I also offer walk-and-talk therapy for those who find movement and nature helpful in the therapeutic process.

Sessions last for 50 minutes and are either long-term or short-term. My fees are £50 after the consultation. I also offer concessionary rates: please enquire.

I am based in both Forest Row and Tunbridge Wells.

I am a registered member of the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (MBACP). I am fully insured, ICO registered, and DBS checked.

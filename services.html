Embarking on the counselling journey can be significant, demonstrating your commitment to personal growth. Ensuring that you find the right therapist is important, so to help you take this step, I offer a valuable 20-minute telephone call and a reduced rate for the first session. This is an opportunity for you to express your counselling goals, ask any questions, and see if you would like us to work together.

I offer flexible therapy tailored to fit into your schedule. In addition to face-to-face counselling, I offer online and telephone counselling, where you can access professional therapeutic support from the comfort of your own space at a time that suits you.

Sessions last for 50 minutes and are either long or short-term. My fees are £35 for the first session and £50 after I also offer concessionary rates.
